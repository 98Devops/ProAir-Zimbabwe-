About ProAir Zimbabwe

Harare's trusted air conditioning, heating and ventilation specialists — making the weather behave since 2012.

Our Story

Making Zimbabwe Comfortable, One Installation at a Time

Established in 2012, ProAir Zimbabwe was founded with a simple mission: to make quality air conditioning and climate control affordable and accessible for every Zimbabwean — whether at home, in the office, or on the road.

Based at 17 Sunderland Avenue, Belvedere, Harare, we have grown from a small team of passionate HVAC technicians into one of Zimbabwe's most trusted air conditioning companies, serving clients in Harare and in all major towns nationwide.

From residential split units and commercial climate systems to car aircon regassing and portable AC hire for events, our team brings expert knowledge, honest pricing and reliable workmanship to every job.
